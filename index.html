What makes a Game Great?

Before we look at what game is the best of all time, we first have to put some focus on what things make a video game great. According to Pluralsight, The 4 things that make a video game great are gameplay, design & graphics, Story, and challenge. We will only focus on gameplay, design & graphics, and story

Gameplay

Gameplay is one of the most important things to a video game because fun gameplay makes a game enjoyable to play and will keep people hooked for longer. Games with great gameplay will hook their users Some videogames with great gameplay are:

•Minecraft

•Fortnite

•Call of Duty

Design & Graphics

Design and graphics are what make a video game pleasing to look at and aesthetic. Players will play a game for longer if it looks good because it is pleasing to them. This artistic style can make a game iconic and its style more well known. Low quality art and graphics are not always a bad thing, though. A game that displays an iconic design is Minecraft. Its pixelated design and low quality graphics are iconoic to the game rather than seeming like a weakness. Some examples of games with great art and graphics are:

•God of War

•Cyberpunk 2077

•Red Dead Redemption 2

Story

A good storyline creates a devout audience and community for a certain game and can increase the longevity of the game series. Great storylines can hook players onto a specific game and keep them playing for a long time. By creating a loyal and devout community, the game franchise can stay afloat and relevant in the gaming industry for a long time. Some great examples of games who have done this are:

•Gears of War

•Call of Duty

•Red Dead Redemption 2

Conclusion

In short, there is not absolute best game out there. There are many different diverse ways that a game can be considered great, and there is no real way to measure which game is the best. Some games have great longevity due to their storylines, while others we dominant in their time of relevance due to their addicting gameplay. There are different ways that a game can be considered great
