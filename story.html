Story

A good storyline creates a devout audience and community for a certain game and can increase the longevity of the game series. Great storylines can hook players onto a specific game and keep them playing for a long time. By creating a loyal and devout community, the game franchise can stay afloat and relevant in the gaming industry for a long time. Some great examples of games who have done this are:

•Gears of War

•Call of Duty

•Red Dead Redemption 2
